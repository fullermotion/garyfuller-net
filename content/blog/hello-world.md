Every site needs a first post, so here's mine.

I built this site as a permanent home for my writing and a professional anchor. The resume and portfolio are here, but the blog is the part I'm most interested in maintaining. Writing forces clarity in a way that thinking alone doesn't.

## What I'll write about

My work sits at the intersection of enterprise architecture, content management, and AI development — so that's where most of the writing will land. Specifically:

- **Adobe Experience Manager** — access design, DAM architecture, AEM as a Cloud Service, governance patterns
- **AI development** — local LLMs with Ollama, hybrid inference architectures, practical applications
- **Modern web development** — SvelteKit, Next.js, deployment patterns, things I've learned the hard way
- **Technical leadership** — what it actually means to architect at the enterprise level

## How this site is built

Stack: SvelteKit, Tailwind CSS, Turso for contact form storage, Resend for email, deployed on Netlify. Blog posts are Markdown files committed directly to the repository — no CMS, no database for content. Simple and durable.
